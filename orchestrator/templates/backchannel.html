📋 Directive type reference

🌩 Scene Directive
Trigger an environmental or atmospheric event. The storm breaks, the candles gutter, an explosion echoes in the distance.

💬 NPC Hint
Have a specific NPC drop a plot-relevant clue. Specify which NPC if possible.

🌍 World Event
Something happens in the wider world right now — news arrives, a crowd forms, soldiers march through.

🎭 Pacing Note
Meta-instruction about tone or dramatic weight. "This is the moment they've been building to — play it big."

↩ Correction
Fix a continuity error or missed clue without railroading. "The players overlooked the bloodstained map — weave it back into view naturally."

⚖ Anti-Railroading Mandate

The GM Engine enforces a hard-coded player agency lock on every narrative.

GM CAN: write NPC reactions, environmental changes, world consequences.

GM CANNOT: write what players think, feel, say, or decide. Every scene ends returning choice to the player.

Your directives shape the world's reactions — not the players' story.
